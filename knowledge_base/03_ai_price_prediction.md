# AI Price Prediction

Dwelleo's AI Price Prediction estimates the fair market value of a property based on location, type, size, condition, amenities, and current market trends. The model is trained on millions of historical transactions and active listings across the KSA and UAE markets.

## How It Works

When you request a prediction, the model analyzes comparable properties sold or listed within the same neighborhood over the past 18 months. It adjusts for property-specific features (floor level, view, finishing quality, age of building) and current demand signals (search volume, average days on market). The output is a predicted price range with a confidence score and a list of comparable properties used in the calculation.

## When to Use It

AI Price Prediction is most useful before publishing a new listing, when negotiating with a seller about realistic pricing, or when reviewing whether an existing listing should be repriced. Agents who use price prediction during listing creation see 23% faster time to sale on average.

## Accuracy and Limitations

The model performs best for standard residential properties (apartments and villas) in well-documented neighborhoods. Accuracy is typically within ±7% for properties in Dubai, Riyadh, Jeddah, and Abu Dhabi. Predictions may be less accurate for unique luxury properties above 20 million AED/SAR, off-plan properties with no recent comparables, commercial properties, or land parcels.

The model does not currently support: agricultural land, historical properties under preservation orders, or properties in newly developed master communities with fewer than 50 transactions.

## Error Messages

If you see "Insufficient comparable data for this property," the neighborhood does not have enough recent transactions to generate a reliable prediction. Try expanding the search radius or contact support if you believe this is a mistake. If you see "Prediction service temporarily unavailable," the AI service is undergoing maintenance — typically resolved within 30 minutes.
