# Refund Policy

This document outlines Dwelleo's refund policy for subscription plans, wallet credits, and add-on purchases.

## Subscription Refunds

### Standard Refund Window

New subscribers can request a full refund within 14 days of their first subscription payment, provided they have not used premium features extensively. "Extensive use" is defined as: more than 10 AI feature calls, more than 25 listings created, or more than 5 featured listing boosts activated.

### Annual Plan Refunds

Customers on annual plans can request a prorated refund for unused months within the first 60 days. After 60 days, annual plans are non-refundable but can be cancelled to prevent renewal.

### No Refund Cases

The following are not eligible for refunds:

- Cancellations made after the standard 14-day window (for monthly plans).
- Plan downgrades — the difference is not refunded.
- Subscription periods where the account was suspended due to policy violations.
- Inactive accounts — non-use is not grounds for refund.

## Wallet Credit Refunds

Unused credit packs can be refunded within 14 days of purchase. Once any credits from a pack have been used, that pack is non-refundable. Refunds for partially used packs are not issued.

Bonus credits (promotional, referral, or plan-included) are never refundable in cash.

## Add-On Purchase Refunds

Individual add-on purchases (Featured Listing Boosts, Virtual Staging, etc.) are non-refundable once activated. If an add-on fails technically (e.g., a boost doesn't appear in search results due to a platform issue), credits are automatically returned to your wallet within 48 hours.

## How to Request a Refund

Email billing@dwelleo.com with:

- Your account email
- Order or invoice number
- Reason for refund

Refund decisions are made within 5 business days. Approved refunds are processed back to the original payment method within 7–14 business days depending on your bank.

## Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision, you can escalate to refunds@dwelleo.com for senior review. Filing a chargeback with your card issuer before contacting us may result in account suspension while the dispute is investigated.

## Special Circumstances

We consider exceptions for: documented medical emergencies, business closure, or platform-side technical failures lasting more than 48 hours. Provide supporting documentation when requesting an exception-based refund.
