# Login and Account Troubleshooting

This guide covers the most common issues users face when accessing their Dwelleo account.

## Can't Log In

If you can't log in, first verify you're using the correct email address. Dwelleo accounts are tied to the email used at signup — using a different email creates a new account.

### Forgot password

Click "Forgot password?" on the login screen. Enter your email and check your inbox for a reset link. The link is valid for 30 minutes. If you don't see the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. Common email providers like Gmail and Outlook occasionally filter Dwelleo emails — adding noreply@dwelleo.com to your contacts prevents this.

### Account locked

After 5 failed login attempts, the account is temporarily locked for 15 minutes as a security measure. Wait and try again, or use the password reset flow. Repeated lockouts may indicate someone is attempting to access your account — change your password immediately and enable two-factor authentication.

### Two-factor authentication issues

If you've lost access to your 2FA device, click "Use recovery code" on the 2FA prompt. If you've lost both, contact support@dwelleo.com with proof of identity (government ID matching the account name). 2FA reset takes 24–48 hours to process for security reasons.

## Email Not Verified

New accounts must verify their email before listing properties. If you didn't receive the verification email, click "Resend verification" on your dashboard. Check spam folders. If verification still fails after 1 hour, contact support.

## Account Suspended

Accounts may be suspended for: violating listing policies (fake listings, duplicate content), payment failures lasting more than 7 days, or reports of fraudulent activity. Suspension notices are sent to your registered email with the specific reason. Appeals can be submitted by replying to the suspension email.

## Changing Email Address

To change your registered email, go to Settings > Account > Email. Enter the new email and confirm via the link sent to the new address. Your old email will receive a notification of the change for security purposes.

## Deleting Your Account

Account deletion is permanent and removes all listings, leads, and transaction history. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Active subscriptions must be cancelled before deletion. Deletion requests are processed within 7 days, after which data cannot be recovered.
