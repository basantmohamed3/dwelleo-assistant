# Supported Regions and Markets

Dwelleo currently operates in two primary markets: the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA) and the United Arab Emirates (UAE). Each market has its own regulatory requirements, payment methods, and supported cities.

## United Arab Emirates

Dwelleo supports listings in all seven emirates, with the strongest coverage in:

- **Dubai** — full coverage, all neighborhoods, AI features fully supported.
- **Abu Dhabi** — full coverage, AI features fully supported.
- **Sharjah** — full coverage, AI features supported with slightly reduced accuracy.
- **Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, Umm Al Quwain** — listings supported, AI features available but with limited comparable data.

All UAE listings require a valid Trakheesi permit number issued by the Dubai Land Department (for Dubai listings) or equivalent authority in other emirates. Listings cannot be published without this number.

## Kingdom of Saudi Arabia

Dwelleo supports listings in major Saudi cities:

- **Riyadh** — full coverage, all districts, AI features fully supported.
- **Jeddah** — full coverage, AI features fully supported.
- **Dammam, Khobar, Dhahran** — full coverage in the Eastern Province.
- **Mecca and Medina** — supported with special compliance requirements; some neighborhoods restricted.
- **NEOM and Red Sea Project** — off-plan listings supported through verified developers only.

All KSA listings require a valid Ejar registration number from the Saudi Ministry of Housing. Foreign ownership restrictions apply in certain zones — the platform automatically flags non-compliant listings.

## Languages

The platform interface is available in English, Arabic, Russian, and Urdu. Listings can be created in any of these languages, and AI-generated descriptions can be translated using the Translation Pack add-on.

## Currency and Payment

UAE listings are priced in AED. KSA listings are priced in SAR. Subscription billing is in USD. Local payment methods supported: Mada (KSA), Apple Pay, Google Pay, Visa, Mastercard, and bank transfer for Enterprise plans.
