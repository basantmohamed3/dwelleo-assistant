# Contact and Support

Dwelleo provides multiple channels for getting help, depending on your plan and the nature of your inquiry.

## Support Channels by Plan

### Free Tier

- Email support: support@dwelleo.com
- Response time: 48–72 business hours
- Self-service help center: help.dwelleo.com

### Starter Plan

- Priority email support: support@dwelleo.com
- Response time: 24 business hours
- Self-service help center

### Professional Plan

- Priority email support: 12 business hours response
- Live chat support: 9 AM – 6 PM GST, Sunday to Thursday
- Self-service help center

### Enterprise Plan

- Dedicated account manager (named contact)
- 24/7 phone support via account manager hotline
- Email and chat: 4 hours response, 24/7
- Quarterly business review meetings

## Business Hours

Our headquarters operates Sunday through Thursday, 9 AM to 6 PM Gulf Standard Time (GST). Live chat and phone support follow these hours unless your plan includes 24/7 support.

## Reporting Issues

For platform bugs, use the "Report a Bug" button in your dashboard footer. Include screenshots and your browser/device info — this gets the issue to engineering fastest. For listing-related disputes (duplicate listings, content violations), use the flag icon on any listing page.

## Languages

Support is available in English, Arabic, and Russian. Urdu support is available via email only.

## What to Include in a Support Request

To get the fastest resolution:

1. Your account email
2. Listing ID (if relevant)
3. Steps to reproduce the issue
4. Screenshots or screen recordings
5. Browser and device information
