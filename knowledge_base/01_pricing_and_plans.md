# Dwelleo Pricing and Subscription Plans

Dwelleo offers four subscription tiers for real estate agents, brokers, and developers operating in the KSA and UAE markets. All prices are listed in USD and billed monthly. Annual billing is available with a 15% discount.

## Free Tier

The Free tier is designed for individual agents getting started on the platform. It includes up to 3 active property listings, basic listing analytics, and standard customer support via email. AI-powered features such as price prediction and listing scoring are not included in the Free tier.

## Starter Plan — $49/month

The Starter plan suits independent agents managing a small portfolio. It includes up to 25 active listings, basic AI price prediction (10 predictions per month), priority email support, and access to the mobile agent app. Wallet credits are not bundled with this plan but can be purchased separately.

## Professional Plan — $149/month

The Professional plan is the most popular choice for active agents and small agencies. It includes up to 150 active listings, unlimited AI price predictions, AI listing scoring (100 scores per month), advanced analytics, lead routing tools, and 500 wallet credits included monthly. Priority chat support is available during business hours (9 AM – 6 PM GST).

## Enterprise Plan — Custom Pricing

The Enterprise plan is designed for large brokerages and property developers. It includes unlimited listings, unlimited AI features, dedicated account management, custom integrations (CRM, ERP), SSO support, and a service-level agreement with 99.9% uptime guarantee. Contact sales@dwelleo.com for a custom quote.

## Plan Changes and Cancellation

Subscribers can upgrade their plan at any time, with prorated billing applied immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle. To cancel, navigate to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Cancellation is effective at the end of the paid period — no partial refunds are issued for unused time. See the refund policy document for exceptions.
