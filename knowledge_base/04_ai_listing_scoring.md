# AI Listing Scoring

AI Listing Scoring evaluates the quality of your property listing on a 0–100 scale and provides actionable suggestions to improve visibility and lead generation. Listings with scores above 80 receive on average 3.4x more inquiries than listings below 50.

## What the Score Measures

The score is calculated across five dimensions:

1. **Photo quality (30%)** — resolution, lighting, composition, number of photos, coverage of key rooms.
2. **Description quality (20%)** — length, keyword relevance, readability, presence of key selling points.
3. **Information completeness (20%)** — filled fields, accurate dimensions, amenities checklist.
4. **Pricing competitiveness (15%)** — comparison to AI price prediction range and similar active listings.
5. **Regulatory compliance (15%)** — valid permit numbers, correct property classification, up-to-date documentation.

## How to Improve Your Score

Each listing receives a personalized improvement report. Common suggestions include:

- Add at least 8 photos covering exterior, living room, kitchen, all bedrooms, bathrooms, and view.
- Replace photos under 1920x1080 resolution.
- Expand the description to 150–300 words and include neighborhood highlights.
- Fill in optional fields like parking spaces, balcony size, and building age.
- Adjust pricing if it falls outside the AI predicted range without justification.

Re-scoring is automatic after any edit and takes 1–2 minutes.

## Accessing the Feature

AI Listing Scoring is available on the Professional plan (100 scores/month) and Enterprise plan (unlimited). Additional scores can be purchased with wallet credits at 3 credits per score. Free and Starter plan users see only the overall score, not the detailed breakdown.

## Score Decay

A listing's score may decrease over time without any edits, due to: new competing listings in the area, market price shifts, or seasonal demand changes. We recommend reviewing scores monthly for active listings.

## Troubleshooting

If your score seems stuck or doesn't update after edits, log out and log back in to refresh the cache. If the issue persists after 24 hours, contact support with your listing ID.
