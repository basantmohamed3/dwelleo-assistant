# Wallet Credits and Add-Ons

Wallet credits are Dwelleo's flexible currency for paying for usage-based features outside your subscription plan. Credits never expire and can be used across any feature in the add-on catalog.

## Credit Pricing

Credits can be purchased in the following packs:

- 100 credits — $19 ($0.19 per credit)
- 500 credits — $89 ($0.18 per credit)
- 1,000 credits — $159 ($0.16 per credit)
- 5,000 credits — $699 ($0.14 per credit)
- 10,000 credits — $1,299 ($0.13 per credit)

Professional plan subscribers receive 500 credits per month as part of their plan, which do roll over month to month.

## What You Can Buy With Credits

The add-on catalog is updated regularly. Current items include:

- **Featured Listing Boost** — 50 credits/day. Pushes your listing to the top of search results for the selected location.
- **AI Price Prediction (extra)** — 5 credits per prediction (for users beyond their plan limit).
- **AI Listing Score (extra)** — 3 credits per score.
- **AI Description Generator** — 2 credits per description.
- **Virtual Staging** — 100 credits per photo.
- **Translation Pack** — 10 credits to translate a listing into all supported languages (Arabic, English, Russian, Urdu).
- **Lead Export to CRM** — 1 credit per lead exported.

## Managing Credits

View your credit balance at Settings > Wallet. The transaction history shows all credit purchases and usage. Auto-recharge can be enabled to automatically purchase a credit pack when your balance drops below a chosen threshold.

## Refunds

Credit purchases are non-refundable once credits have been used. Unused credit purchases can be refunded within 14 days of purchase by contacting billing@dwelleo.com.

## Enterprise Credit Pools

Enterprise customers can set up shared credit pools across team members with role-based spending limits. Contact your account manager to enable this feature.
